# Privacy Policy — BalWealth

Effective date: 2026-05-10

This document describes how the **BalWealth** application
(`com.empiretycoon.game`) handles your information.

## TL;DR

**BalWealth does NOT collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data.**
The game runs entirely on your device and does not require an internet
connection.

## What data is stored

BalWealth stores a single save file on the local storage of your Android
device:

- Path: `/data/data/com.empiretycoon.game/files/save.json`
- Contents: your in-game progress (cash, buildings, employees, stocks,
  achievements, settings).
- This file never leaves your device.

If Android Auto Backup is enabled in your device settings, the save file
may be backed up to your personal Google Drive by the Android system
itself. Anthropic, the developer, never has access to this backup. You
can disable auto-backup in your device settings.

## What data is NOT collected

We do not collect any of the following:

- Your name, email, phone number, or any contact info.
- Device identifiers (advertising ID, IMEI, MAC, IP).
- Location data.
- Photos, contacts, calendars, microphone, or camera.
- Telemetry, crash reports, analytics, or usage statistics.

## Permissions

BalWealth requests only a single Android permission:

- `android.permission.VIBRATE` — used for haptic feedback during certain
  gameplay events. No data leaves the device because of this permission.

The app does NOT request internet permission.

## Children

BalWealth is rated for all ages. We do not knowingly collect data from
anyone, including children, because we do not collect data at all.

## Third-party services

BalWealth includes no third-party SDKs, advertising frameworks, analytics,
crash reporters, or social integrations. The only network code in the app
is for Google Play Store updates handled by the Android system itself.

## Changes to this policy

If a future version of BalWealth ever introduces data collection, this
policy will be updated and the change will be communicated through the
app's release notes on the Play Store. Until then, this single statement
remains the entire privacy footprint of the app.

## Contact

For privacy-related questions, please open an issue on the project
repository: https://github.com/xandru582/balwealth/issues
